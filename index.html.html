Introduction & Context

The I International Congress of Interactive Mathematical Conferences, organized by UNAD, took place on April 20, 2026, via YouTube Live. The congress gathered national speakers with recognized academic trajectories, focused on UNAD's curricular axes of Logic, Algebra and Calculus — fostering active, student-centered learning through interactive and gamified methodologies.

"The event will feature national speakers with academic trajectory and experience in mathematical education, addressing topics articulated to the formative axes of the Curricular Network of Logic, Algebra and Calculus, through interactive tools and gamified dynamics." — Official description · UNAD YouTube Channel

The I International Congress of Interactive Mathematical Conferences (UNAD, 2026) demonstrated that mathematics education thrives when traditional rigor combines with dynamic, interactive methodologies.
